M A P L E   C I R C L E

Maple Circle is a platform established to foster connections what we call ‘Networking’ which mean informal community: the Casual Connection Hub.

Its purpose is to provide international students, regardless of their country of origin, with diverse opportunities to build these connections. By doing so, Maple Circle increases their options for social interaction and significantly enhances their safety during use.
